Overview

About Us.

Save your Time, Money and Efforts. Investing is now Easier, Simpler and Better with us

Our Mission - We at InvestoAsia aim to make a product that can enable all classes of investors from anywhere in the world to be able to invest in emerging economies.
InvestoAsia envisions to Democratise Access to Emerging Markets through our innovation-driven blockchain ready thematic investment solutions. We want to change the age old tradition of investments which not only involves hefty fees but is also extremely complex and bureaucratic by making the process of investments easier, simpler, better.

Know More

Our Features:

24/7 Operational

Doesn’t matter if the market is open or closed, you can access from anywhere in the world, anyday, anytime!

Fractional Ownership

IAF tokens represent a part of the asset, hence you can invest in any fractions and any multiples. There is no minimum investment limit.

Enhanced Liquidity

Tokens can be traded globally and anytime. This increased scalability creates greater liquidity in even illiquid assets.

Thematic Funds

We create funds that are customisable, reflect an idea, theme or objective from a country, making investmenting easier so that you don’t have to rack your brains or hire another one!

Easy Onboarding

No paperwork, no hassle. Just 2 minutes for getting registered on the platform and you are all set to invest!

Cross-border Payments

With our remittance partners spread across 200 countries, we enable instant payments both in fiat and cryptocurrencies.

Low Cost

The fees involved is negligible so as to accomplish the mission of making investing more affordable to a wider group of investors.
